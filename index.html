      The rules that keep your skin happy

      Vitamin C in the morning, retinol at night — never together. And never retinol + salicylic acid the same night (the schedule alternates them for you).
      SPF every single morning is what makes the vitamin C and retinol both safe and effective. Salicylic acid: just the nose / T-zone. Stinging or peeling = too much, too fast — ease off, then rebuild.
